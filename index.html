About Us

Apple product lines

Apple Inc. (formerly Apple Computer Inc.) is an American computer and consumer electronics company famous for creating the iPhone, iPad and Macintosh computers. Apple is one of the largest companies globally with a market cap of more than $3 trillion.

Read More

Mac Computer

The Macintosh, or Mac, is Apple's line of personal computers. Its main product types are the iMac all-in-one desktop, Mac mini standard desktop, Mac Studio media-focused desktop, Mac Pro professional production workstation desktop, MacBook Air consumer laptop and MacBook Pro professional laptop.

iPhone and iPad

The iPhone is Apple's smartphone line, and the iPad is its tablet. The iPhone was the first smartphone to receive mass market adoption due to its ease of use, built-in app store and capacitive multitouch touchscreen.

Apple Watch

The Apple Watch is a smartwatch and fitness tracker. It can monitor health signals, such as ECG, blood oxygen and heart rate data. It can also act as a digital key, allowing users to unlock, lock and start compatible cars.

Apple AirPods

AirPods are wireless headphones that sync with Apple devices through Bluetooth. Apple users can listen to audio including music, movies and messages through AirPods. Additionally, users can take phone calls through AirPods and use Siri. The AirPods Pro 2 can be used as an over-the-counter hearing aid for those who do not suffer from severe hearing loss, but to use this feature, users need to take a test to analyze hearing loss.

On-device processing

The cornerstone of Apple Intelligence is on‑device processing. We’ve integrated it deep into your iPhone, iPad and Mac, and throughout your apps, so it’s aware of your personal data, without collecting your personal data. This is only possible through Apple’s unique integration of hardware and software, and years-long investment in building advanced silicon for on‑device intelligence.

Private Cloud Compute

When you make a request, Apple Intelligence analyses whether it can be processed on the device. If it requires greater computational capacity, it can draw on Private Cloud Compute and send only the data relevant to your task to be processed on Apple silicon–based servers. Your data is never made accessible to Apple — it’s used exclusively to fulfill your requests. And independent experts can inspect the code that runs on the servers to verify our privacy promise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our app, subscription plans, features, and troubleshooting. Our FAQ section helps you quickly resolve issues and get the most out of your experience.

1. What is the Premium Subscription?

The Premium Subscription offers additional features like advanced analytics, more customization options, and an ad-free experience.

2. How can I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, go to the App Store on your device, tap on your profile picture, then select Subscriptions. From there, find the app in your list of subscriptions and select Cancel Subscription. You won’t be charged for the next billing cycle.

3. Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for all new users. During this period, you’ll have access to all premium features. After the trial ends, you can choose to subscribe or continue using the free version of the app.

4. Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes, as long as you are logged into the same Apple ID, you can use the app across multiple iOS devices. Your subscription will be available on all devices connected to your Apple ID.

5. How do I restore my purchases on a new device?

To restore your purchases, open the app and go to the Settings section. Tap Restore Purchases and follow the instructions. Ensure you’re logged into the same Apple ID on your new device.
